Trien lam chuyen de Dau an van hoa Cham o Lam Dong se chinh thuc khai mac luc 16 gio ngay 24/7 tai Bao tang Dieu khac Cham Da Nang. Chuong trinh do Bao tang Dieu khac Cham Da Nang phoi hop voi Bao tang tinh Lam Dong to chuc, gioi thieu khoang 30 hien vat cung 100 hinh anh, tu lieu khoa hoc.

Trien lam tap trung vao hai chu de chinh: Di san kien truc - dieu khac Cham tai Lam Dong va Doi song van hoa, tin nguong cua nguoi Cham tai Lam Dong. Sau khi sap xep don vi hanh chinh, cac vung cu tru truyen thong cua nguoi Cham thuoc tinh Binh Thuan truoc day nay thuoc Lam Dong, tao nen khong gian van hoa Cham phong phu voi hon 43.300 nguoi Cham sinh song.

Phan trung bay ngoai san vuon gioi thieu he thong 9 di tich kien truc Cham cap tinh va cap quoc gia, noi bat la cum thap Po Sah Inu, thap Po Dam, cac thanh duong Hoi giao Bani, cung nhieu phe tich den thap va phat hien khao co hoc tieu bieu.

Dac biet, trien lam gioi thieu nhieu hien vat quy dang luu giu tai Bao tang tinh Lam Dong, trong do co Bo suu tap di san Hoang toc Cham voi cac vuong mien bang vang cua vua va hoang hau Cham duoc che tac tinh xao. Cung xuat hien la hai Bao vat quoc gia gom Linga bang vang phat hien tai thap Po Dam va tuong Avalokitesvara phat hien tai Bac Binh.

Ben canh di san vat the, trien lam con tai hien doi song van hoa, tin nguong cua cong dong nguoi Cham qua hai nhom Cham Balamon (Ahier) va Cham Bani (Awal), cung cac nghi le vong doi va le hoi truyen thong tieu bieu nhu Kate, Ramawan, Rija Nagar.

Mot diem nhan khac la khong gian gioi thieu cac nghe thu cong truyen thong, dac biet la nghe det va nghe gom - nhung nghe van dang duoc cong dong nguoi Cham gin giu, trao truyen qua nhieu the he, huong toi muc tieu bao ton di san gan voi phat trien du lich ben vung.
