Thu Sau ngay 24/7 la mot tuan bung no cua nhac quoc te voi 22 album va EP ra mat cung luc, theo tong hop cua Official Charts. Nhieu ten tuoi lon dong loat tro lai, hua hen cuoc dua gay can tren cac bang xep hang Anh.

Shania Twain tro lai voi album phong thu thu bay Little Miss Twain, duoc gioi thieu la tac pham ca nhan nhat cua co, dua nguoi nghe ve nhung nam thang tuoi tre voi chat rock va R&B pha lan am huong dong que dac trung. Album co su gop giong cua nu hoang nhac dong que Tanya Tucker.

Charli XCX mo rong de che sang tao voi Music, Fashion, Film - su hoa tron giua van hoa dai chung, danh tieng va tieng guitar day nang luong, voi cac ban hit Rock Music, SS26 va Camera. Neu vuon toi ngoi vi quan quan, day se la album Quan quan thu tu tai Anh cua co.

Ban nhac huyen thoai New York The Strokes chinh thuc tai xuat voi Reality Awaits, album phong thu dau tien ke tu The New Abnormal nam 2020, duoc thu am tai Costa Rica cung nha san xuat Rick Rubin. Trong khi do, Snow Patrol phat hanh phien ban ky niem 20 nam album Eyes Open voi 36 ca khuc.

Ngoi sao Nam Phi Tyla tung ra album phong thu thu hai theo phong cach amapiano-pop day tu tin, voi single trieu view CHANEL va man ket hop cung Zara Larsson. WILLOW tro lai voi The Thread, con Waterparks mang den album thu sau JINX day mau sac pop-punk.

Tuan phat hanh cung danh dau cot moc Bang xep hang Album chinh thuc cua Anh tron 70 tuoi vao ngay 22/7 - dip Snow Patrol tro thanh nghe si dau tien duoc vinh danh o Dai Danh vong (Hall of Fame) cua Official Charts.

O mang single, nhom nhac nu toan cau KATSEYE gay chu y voi ca khuc Animal, trong khi cac ten tuoi nhu Sam Smith, Pitbull ket hop Jason Derulo, Victoria Monet va Morgan Wallen cung dong loat tung ban moi.
