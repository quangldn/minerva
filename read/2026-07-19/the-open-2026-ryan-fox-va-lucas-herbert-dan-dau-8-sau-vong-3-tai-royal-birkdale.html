The third round of the Open Championship is under way at Royal Birkdale in Southport, England, and there are storylines galore to follow after an eventful second round of the last men’s major tournament this year.

Lucas Herbert and Sam Burns shot 62s to tie a major championship record.

Bryson DeChambeau was penalized two shots for inadvertently affecting path of his swing on the fifth hole. Jon Rahm was given an official conduct warning for throwing a club, and Masters winner Rory McIlroy scrambled to make the cut.

Herbert holds a two-shot lead at 8-under over Jackson Huber and Ryan Gerard entering Saturday.

Broadcast and streaming coverage of the third round will run from 7 a.m. to 3 p.m. ET on NBC and Peacock.
